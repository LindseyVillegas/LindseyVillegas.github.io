Metro

About

This small coffee shop is one of the coffee shops located directly on the University of Washington - Tacoma campus. Featuring local artwork, this coffee shop offers great coffee at decent prices.

Pro's and Con's

Pro's

Metro coffee has friendly baristas, the kind that memorize your order if you come in often enough. Combining good coffee and decent prices, this coffee shop is a favorite among students. Located in the middle of campus, this coffee shop has a nice atmosphere for studying.

Con's

Metro is a small coffee shop with limited seating, and limited amounts of outlets. It also has limited hours and is closed on weekends.
