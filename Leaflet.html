Lab 3: Leaflet Maps - Bicycle Routes in Fairfax County

** Click on the map to enable zoom with mouse wheel. **

With the concerns related to global warming, more people are finding ways to reduce their carbon footprint. One way is to use public transportation. Another option is to ride bicycles; however bicycles present their own safety issues.

These maps look at the bicycle routes throughout Fairfax County in Virginia in relation to a few places that people travel. First, we look at bicycle routes to schools and colleges, and next we look at the ability to travel by bicycle to community centers and human services offices.

Bicycle Routes, Schools, and Colleges

This map shows the locations of schools and colleges. If you click on each point it will give information on the school. You can also click on the roads to see information on the safety and how fast cars travel on that road.

Bicycle Routes, Community Centers, and Human Service Locations

This map shows the locations of Community Centers and Human Service Locations. If you click on each point it will give information on the point. You can also click on the roads to see information on the safety and how fast cars travel on that road.
