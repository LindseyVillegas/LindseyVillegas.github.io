Metronome

About

Metronome is located off of 6th Avenue in downtown Tacoma. This coffee shop is located along a popular avenue that is also the home of popular restaurants and nightlife.

Pro's and Con's

Pro's

Metronome features good coffee and food, such as pastries, breakfast sandwiches, and even pancakes. This coffee shop has a good amount of seating, but is a little limited on outlet access. Metronome also has events such as live music.

Con's

One of the biggest downfalls of Metronome is that it has very limited parking behind the building and on the street. It can also get a bit loud due to the amount of people in this coffee shop.
